# Cold Outreach Notes — Central Texas Business Enrichment

## Priority Categories

### 🔥 AI Receptionist Pitch (High Call Volume)
These businesses live and die by the phone. Missed calls = missed revenue.
- **Plumbers** — Emergency calls at all hours; owner is often on a job site
- **Electricians** — Same-day service requests; can't answer while on a ladder
- **Pest Control** — Seasonal surge calls; small teams stretched thin
- **Dentists** — Front desk juggling patients, insurance, and phone simultaneously
- **Law Firms** — Potential clients call once and move on if no answer
- **Auto Repair** — Techs double as phone staff; loud shop environment

### 🌐 New Website Pitch
After enrichment, filter for `website_modern = "none"` or `"no"`. Top categories likely to have outdated or missing sites:
- **Lawn Care** — Many solo operators with no web presence at all
- **Barber/Salon** — Often rely solely on walk-ins or social media
- **Feed Stores** — Rural-oriented; websites often static HTML from 2010s
- **Auto Repair** — Shops that have run on word-of-mouth for decades
- **Gun Stores** — Smaller FFL dealers with no site or a basic listing page
- **Storage** — Many mom-and-pop facilities with outdated sites

### 🤖 Automation Pitch (Booking, Reminders, Follow-ups)
These categories benefit most from automated scheduling, reminders, and CRM workflows:
- **Restaurants** — Reservation management, online ordering, review follow-up
- **Pet Services** — Boarding/grooming booking, vaccination reminders, waitlists
- **Realtors** — Lead capture, drip campaigns, showing scheduling
- **Insurance** — Policy renewal reminders, quote follow-up sequences
- **Spa/Nail Salons** — Appointment booking, no-show management, rebooking prompts
- **Dentists** — Recall reminders, cancellation backfill, new patient onboarding
